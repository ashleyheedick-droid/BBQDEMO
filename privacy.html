Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Meridian Technologies ("we," "us," or "our") operates the DineIQ platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our Service, both as a restaurant partner and as a restaurant customer interacting with a DineIQ-powered system.

1. Information We Collect

Restaurant Partners (You)

  • Contact information: name, email, phone number
  • Business information: restaurant name, location, cuisine type, seating capacity
  • Account credentials and access codes
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment provider)

Restaurant Customers (Your Guests)

  • Waitlist information: name, phone number, party size, seating preferences, allergy notes
  • Feedback data: star ratings, written feedback, email (if provided)
  • Chat interactions: questions asked to the AI concierge
  • Staff shoutouts: messages and staff names

2. How We Use Information

Data TypePurpose
Waitlist dataManaging wait times, SMS notifications, seating operations
Phone numbersSending waitlist SMS notifications (with opt-in consent only)
Feedback dataDisplaying on manager dashboard, sentiment analysis, service improvement
Chat logsImproving AI responses, analytics, identifying common customer questions
Contact info (partners)Account management, support, billing, service communications

3. SMS Communications

  • SMS messages are sent only to customers who explicitly opt in via the waitlist form.
  • Messages are limited to waitlist status updates (table ready notifications).
  • Customers can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.
  • We do not send marketing messages to customer phone numbers unless separately consented.
  • Phone numbers are not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored using Google Cloud infrastructure (Google Sheets and Google Apps Script).
  • All data transmission occurs over HTTPS (encrypted in transit).
  • Access to restaurant data is restricted to authorized personnel and the restaurant partner.
  • We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures.
  • Manager dashboards are protected by access codes configurable by the restaurant partner.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties that help us operate the platform (Google Cloud, SMS providers), subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to affected parties.

6. Data Ownership and Retention

  • Restaurant partners own their customer data. We process it on your behalf.
  • Upon account termination, we will provide data export and delete our copies within 30 days.
  • Customer waitlist data is retained for the duration of the subscription for analytics purposes.
  • Customers may request deletion of their personal data by contacting the restaurant or us directly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

8. Cookies and Tracking

DineIQ uses localStorage (browser-based storage) for configuration settings and session management. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels. No data is shared with advertising networks.

9. Children's Privacy

DineIQ is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify restaurant partners of material changes via email. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
